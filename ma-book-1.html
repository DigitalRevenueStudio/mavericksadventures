About the Book
A hide-and-seek adventure
your little ones will love

Maverick is on the move — but where has he gone? Children follow along room by room, place by place, searching for their favourite cockapoo in this charming hide-and-seek adventure.

With bouncy rhymes that beg to be read aloud and illustrations full of warmth and colour, Where Is Maverick? turns every storytime into a shared moment your little one will ask for again and again.

The story gently teaches children about curiosity, problem-solving, and the joy of discovery — all wrapped up in the kind of simple, engaging narrative that works brilliantly at bedtime or during the day.

And the best part? Maverick is real. A real cockapoo, a real character, and a real adventure — brought to life for children everywhere.
