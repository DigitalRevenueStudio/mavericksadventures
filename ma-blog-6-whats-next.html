What's Next for Maverick?

📅 13th March 2026 ✍️ Digit 🏷️ Behind the Scenes

When we published Where Is Maverick? a few months ago, we honestly had no idea what to expect. A children's book about a real cockapoo, self-published, built from scratch — it felt like a leap into the unknown.

But the response has been wonderful. Families have been buying it, reading it, and coming back to tell us their little ones ask for it every single night. That's everything we hoped for.

So — what happens next?

"Maverick is just getting started. There are more adventures to come, and we want you to be part of every single one."

The website is live

Big news first — mavericksadventures.co.uk is now live. After months of building in the background, the site is up, the books page is live, and you can now explore everything Maverick's Adventures has to offer in one place.

We've kept it warm and simple — just like the books. You'll find the full story behind Maverick, details on Book 1, and a free activity pack you can download right now if you sign up below.

Book 2 is on its way

We won't give too much away just yet, but Book 2 is already in the works. Maverick has more places to explore, more rooms to disappear into, and more adventures waiting just around the corner.

We've been asking our readers what they'd love to see next — a beach adventure, a farm adventure, or something else entirely. The response has been brilliant, and it's helping us shape exactly what Book 2 will look like.

If you want to be the first to know when it launches, sign up below. We'll send you early access, and you'll be among the first to read it.
