What Data We Collect and Why

Email sign-ups: When you sign up to receive our free activity pack or newsletter, we collect your name and email address. We use this information solely to send you the content you signed up for — the free activity pack, updates about new books and occasional news from Maverick's Adventures. We will never sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Website analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website. This collects anonymised information including which pages you visit, how long you spend on them and where you arrived from. This data does not personally identify you. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Your rights under GDPR: If you are based in the UK or EU, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to request its deletion, and to unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. To request access to or deletion of your data, please contact us via the website.
