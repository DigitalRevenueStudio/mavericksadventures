Dog walks with Maverick are one of the best parts of the day. Fresh air, a happy cockapoo investigating every interesting smell, and the quiet satisfaction of a dog who has had a proper run around.

The less glamorous part of dog ownership — picking up after them — is just a fact of life. You are going to do it several times a day, every day, for the entire life of your dog. It makes sense to use something that actually works.

We switched to Earth Rated lavender bags a while back and have not looked at anything else since. Here is the honest version of why.

“Once you've had a bag split on you, you start caring quite a lot about bag quality.”

The Problem with Cheap Bags

Most dog owners will recognise this: you buy a bulk roll of the cheapest poo bags you can find, because they are all the same really, aren't they? Then one splits. Usually at exactly the wrong moment, in exactly the wrong conditions.

Beyond the obvious practical problem, cheap bags smell exactly like what they contain. You are carrying it for the rest of the walk. Not ideal.

We went through a few different options before landing on Earth Rated. Some were fine. Some were not. Earth Rated are the ones we keep buying.

Why Earth Rated Lavender

The lavender scent is the first thing most people notice. It is not overpowering — it is genuinely subtle, just enough to make the whole thing considerably more pleasant than it would otherwise be. On a cold morning walk that small detail makes a real difference.

The bags themselves are thick. Properly thick — the kind where you tie them off and forget about it rather than handling them with the caution of someone defusing something. They tear off the roll cleanly, open easily with cold fingers, and have never once let us down.

Each roll is 15 bags. We keep one dispenser on Maverick's lead and a spare roll in every coat we own. At this point it is just part of the routine.

The Part That Actually Matters

Maverick has no opinion on poo bags. He is immediately interested in the next thing. But for everyone else on the walk, the right bag makes a genuinely noticeable difference to the experience.
