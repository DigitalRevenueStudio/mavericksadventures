The Real Dog Behind the Books

This is Maverick

Maverick is a cockapoo with a huge personality, an insatiable curiosity and an absolute gift for finding trouble in the most endearing ways possible. He is the real dog behind every story in the Maverick's Adventures series.

Every book starts with something Maverick actually did — a hiding spot, an adventure, a new discovery — and builds a story around it. That is what makes the books feel so warm and lived-in. The adventures are real.

He loves exploring, meeting new friends and is remarkably patient with being the subject of a children's book series. He has taken it all in his stride.
